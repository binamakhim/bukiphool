### there….under my pillow…

there….  
just under my pillow  
where,  
once used to be  
My achievements,  
my dreams,  
my to-dos  
and sweet sleep...  
Now I find only  
paracetamol,  
Pain killer,  
reasons of  
insomnia and tears!
