A poem of a flower named Tall Pearly Everlasting, bukiphool in Nepal <!--more--> 

---
Bukiphool in wild  

Rose in garden  
Lotus in pond  
and Cactus in desert!

She is not only a Wildflower or  
just a Homegrown flower...  
She is more than that!  
She is more than  
you know…

Yes, SHE is!
